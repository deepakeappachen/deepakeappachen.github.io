Research Summary

I am an observational astrophysicist working in time-domain astronomy. My research interests primarily focus on the host studies and follow-up of fast X-ray transients in the optical wavelength. I have also worked on the electromagnetic follow-up of gravitational waves using Gaia. Currently, I work on the multiwavelength follow-up of transients with the GROWTH-India telescope, Indian Astronomical Observatory and Giant Metrewave Radio Telescope .
